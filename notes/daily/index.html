things I do daily [live]

03/02/2024

These are the things I’ve committed to or keep coming back to. I wonder if these distract from bigger projects, after all, if I keep adding these small dailies my todos will eventually be filled but as for now I will keep these that bring me joy.

  • morning pages: 3 page stream of consciousness journaling in the mornings
  • painting a snippet of the sky: the gray weather depressed me so I decided to paint it every day. now I notice so much in the sky that I’m endlessly fascinated regardless of the weather.
  • a poem a day keeps the blues at bay: it’s surprising how much five minutes a day can ground me.
  • 20 min dance session at night: I wiggle and wave like a crazy person before I sleep. this is a pillar of my happiness.
