Drawing for Designers

2020

Sketches from MIT's Drawing for Designers studio class. Studies in movement, perspective, and lighting.

This was the semester interruped by the pandemic, leading to imaginative sketches, blending the real and the remembered. Many sketches were done from my room, adding elements of fantasy to transform the space into a space for speculation.
